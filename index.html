Audio: It's Notts Just Physio Podcast

Qualified sound engineer with a BSc (Hons), I have recently acted as producer on the "It's Notts Just Physio" podcast. This podcast has been instrumental in bridging the gap between staff and student relationships post-COVID, as well as acting as a platform for staff and students to promote their work and as a learning resource.

  • 30+ episodes
  • 3,000 listens
  • Accessed worldwide
  • Platform for staff onboarding
  • Presented at annual Teaching and Learning Festival
  • Used by students for CPD
  • Cited by potential students and staff in interviews
‘It’s Notts Just Physio’ represents a distinctive pedagogical resource within the BSc Physiotherapy curriculum, fostering a sense of identity and community among students. The ‘Welcome Week’ podcast playlist facilitates the integration of new students, while the broader series encompasses learning materials, career guidance, and wellbeing support, positioning staff as accessible role models.
