About Me

I’m a digital creator with a BSc (Hons) in Sound Engineering and Multimedia, specialising as a Learning Technologist. My expertise spans instructional design, accessibility and UX (WCAG/PSBAR compliance), and audio/video production. I collaborate with subject matter experts to design and deliver interactive, engaging learning experiences.

Alongside my creative and technical expertise, I hold a PRINCE2 certification in project management and am a Certified Member of ALT (CMALT), evidencing my commitment to professional practice in learning technology. I am also trained in Mental Health First Aid, which supports my ability to foster inclusive and supportive learning environments.

Audio Engineer

I am a qualified sound engineer with a BSc (Hons), I currently lead production on the It's Notts Just Physio podcast. This project has been instrumental in bridging the gap between theory and practice within the BSc Physiotherapy curriculum, while also serving as a platform for staff onboarding and continuing professional development and is available on all streaming platforms.

Video Production

I have extensive experience in filming and editing educational videos that are integrated into interactive learning packages. These resources are designed to enhance student engagement, improve accessibility, and provide flexible learning opportunities within the curriculum.

In addition, I produce short-form content to create concise and digestible learning materials. These “learning shorts” are optimised for platforms such as YouTube and social media, providing students with accessible, on-the-go content that complements longer-form resources.


Web Design

Design of a bespoke "Virtual Placement" VLE in response to shortage of student placements. Students are introduced to their practice supervisor Krish who guides them along their learning journey where they will complete home visits to a virtual patient Lionel.

This project applies experiential learning principles and incorporates feedback loops that replicate the guidance of mentors in clinical placements. It leverages AJAX, jQuery, JavaScript, PHP, CSS, and HTML to create a rich, interactive learning experience. The project has now been rolled out to three student cohorts, with further development shaped by their feedback. In Summer 2025, I conducted an accessibility audit using the WAVE, axe DevTools and Silktide plug-ins to ensure compliance and enhance usability.
